# Irondale Marching Knights — Weekly Update

**July 15, 2026**

**Band Camp Edition**

## Sunday, July 19

Students should report to the Irondale Auditorium at 1:30 pm on Sunday July 19th. They should bring the following items…
- Luggage (see the [Band Camp Handbook](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1SFS1FaIr80BwOw0T2gogyYt-EYztSrJaLdrMKNuX8h8/edit?usp=sharing) for a complete packing list)
  - There are no elevators in the dorms, whatever your kid brings must be carried up the stairs
- Instrument / Equipment
- Extra Supplies (extra valve oil, reeds, cork grease, etc.)
- Music Binder + Pencil
- Spending $$ (late night pizzas, coffee at the student center, campus bookstore, etc.)
- Cell Phone + Charger
- Medication
  - Controlled substances must be in the original container, placed inside a Ziploc bag labeled with the students' first and last name, with written instructions inside the Ziploc bag. All controlled substances will be submitted to Dr. L before loading the buses.

## Free Time & Student Activities

Students will get to enjoy free time every day from 12:30-2:00pm and 10:00-11:30pm. The recreational pool will be available to students on Wednesday during the afternoon block. The pool will have lifeguards on duty.

Students are encouraged to bring cards/board games, puzzles, books, frisbees, and other things to enjoy alone or with friends. Avoid bringing valuables such as expensive jewelry or gaming consoles.

## The Keys to Success

- Go to bed. Get your sleep.
- Drink plenty of water.
- Eat 3 good meals per day.
- Always give 100% effort.
- Be kind and courteous (say "please" and "thank you").
- Be prepared and ready to go.
