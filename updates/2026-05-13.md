## What To Bring & Wear To Spring Training

Students should bring their marching instrument / music, any other equipment they use at practice, and a water bottle. Wear athletic clothes and close- toed athletic shoes. We recommend bringing sunglasses, bug spray, and sunscreen in case we go outside.
